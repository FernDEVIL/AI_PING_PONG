AI PING PONG GAME





Instructions

  1. First of all you should keep your laptop/computer straight.

  2. You should move about 3-4 feet away from your laptop/computer

  3. Move your right wrist in an ascending and descending order, a red dot should appear on your right wrist.

  4. Press the Play Game button and again move 3-4 feet away from your laptop/computer

  5. The red paddle is your paddle which will move according to your wrist movement.

  6. Then enjoy :)
